About us

KingPalloza is an annual five-day music festival in buenos aires, palermo, at bosque de palermo, founded in 2001 and since expanded to other countries, like United states, Italy, Japan, France, etc. Performances include but are not limited to alternative rock, K-pop, pop ,punk rock, hip hop, and electronic music. KingPalooza was conceived and created in 2001 by Tonny Kinge. The first KingPalooza tour had a diverse collection of bands and was a commercial success. It stopped in more than twenty cities in America. In 2019, Spin rated the first KingPalooza as the best one in a list of "The 35 Greatest Concerts of the Last 35 Years.
